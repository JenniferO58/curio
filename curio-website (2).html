Behind every box

How the curation actually works

01

A local curator leads every box

Each country has a dedicated food curator — a chef, food writer, or culinary historian based in that country. They propose the items, write the cultural context, and approve every final selection. Your Nigeria box was built by someone who grew up eating jollof.

02

Every product is tasted and tested

Nothing goes in a box without being tasted by the Curiō team first. We reject products that don't meet the brief — even well-known brands. The test is simple: does this genuinely represent the food culture of this country?

03

The box tells a coherent story

Items aren't chosen in isolation. The frozen meal, the street snack, the pantry staple, the dessert — they all connect to the same food culture and work together in your kitchen. Every box is a full cultural experience, not a random selection.

04

Sourced directly where possible

We work with independent producers, small food businesses, and family-run suppliers in each country wherever we can. When that's not feasible, we work with the most authentic UK-based importers. We never substitute authenticity for convenience.
